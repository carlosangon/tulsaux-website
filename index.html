About

Tulsa UX meets the second Tuesday each month at 36°N to learn about the latest trends, hear from industry professionals in our community and discuss best practices!

Mission

The Tulsa UX user group unites Tulsa’s user-experience practitioners in order to expand our skills and improve our community’s experience.
